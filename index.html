Powder coating is extremely resistant to chipping, flaking, solvents, corrosives, and general wear and tear. This durability makes powder coating a superior protective coating for all metal products. Each piece brought to us is prepared through chemical stripping, degreasing, or sandblasting to ensure an absolutely clean surface ready for coating. The coating is evenly applied in a powder form and then baked, resulting in a hard, smooth, paint-like finish that will bend with the metal if dented or struck. The versatile properties of powder coating make it ideal for a variety of needs, both decorative & practical.
